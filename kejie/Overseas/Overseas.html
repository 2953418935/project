科捷海外战略

OVERSEAS STRATEGY

中国政府提出建设“新丝绸之路经济带”和“21世纪海上丝绸之路”的合作倡议，即一带一路倡议之后，科捷积极开拓沿线国家的业务合作，帮助中国企业走出国门，进入日益增长的全球市场。

Following The Silk Road Economic Belt and the 21st-century Maritime Silk Road, known as The Belt and Road Initiative (BRI), proposed by the Chinese government, ITL actively develops business cooperation in these countries to help Chinese companies to enter the fast-growing global market.

海外服务内容

OVERSEAS SERVICES

作为供应链服务专家，科捷旨在向中国或当地企业在“一带一路”沿线国家提供专业的国际贸易、电子商务、仓储运输、清关服务等综合供应链解决方案。

As a professional supply chain service provider, ITL is committed to providing solutions and services, such as international trade, e-commerce, warehousing, delivery and custom clearance, to Chinese and local companies in countries participating in the Belt and Road Initiative
